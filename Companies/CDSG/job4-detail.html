Capital Diamond Star Group (CDSG)

Product Manager (Citizens Pay)

Pabedan | Yangon
500,000 MMK
Job description

Owned the end-to-end product development process, from ideation to launch, ensuring alignment with business goals. Explore new product opportunities based on user research, insights, and industry trends. Lead initiatives to improve user retention through targeted strategies and engagement programs. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and prioritize growth opportunities. Develop user stories and requirements, prioritize stories, epics, and themes aligned with the product strategy.

Responsibility
Understanding and representing user needs.
Monitoring the market and developing competitive analyses.
Defining a vision for a product.
Aligning stakeholders around the vision for the product.
Prioritizing product features and capabilities.
Qualifications
Willingness to experiment, learn new ways of working and new and technologies.
Strong strategic and analytical skills combined with consultancy skills and robust understanding of the business planning cycle and processes.
Strong business acumen, including a good understating of marketing, market trends and customers in the market.
Minimum 2 years of experiences in related field.
Prefer experiences in consumer product handling and product launching.
Open to
Male/Female
