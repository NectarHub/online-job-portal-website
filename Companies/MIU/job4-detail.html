Restaurant Manager

Chanayetharzan | Mandalay
500,000 MMK
Job description

Efficiently coordinate daily operations of both Front of the House and Back of the House within the restaurant. Organize and supervise shifts to ensure seamless workflow and customer service. Deliver exceptional service to maximize customer satisfaction. Respond to customer complaints promptly and accurately, aiming for resolution and positive customer experiences. Regularly review product quality and conduct research to identify and evaluate potential new vendors. Estimate future needs for goods, kitchen utensils, and cleaning products. Appraise staff performance and provide constructive feedback to enhance productivity.

Responsibility
Coordinating daily restaurant management operations
Delivering superior food and beverage service and maximizing customer satisfaction
Responding efficiently and accurately to restaurant customer complaints
Organize and supervise shifts
Estimate future needs for goods, kitchen utensils and cleaning products
Qualifications
Proven experience 3 years and above in restaurant management or a related field.
Strong leadership and organizational skills.
Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
In-depth knowledge of food industry trends and best practices.
Ability to work under pressure and adapt to dynamic operational environments.
