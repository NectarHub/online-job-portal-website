Yoma Bank

Assistant Manager - Trade Finance Sales & Advisory

Thanlyin | Yangon
800,000 MMK
Job description

Trade & FX Sales Specialist, focus on driving the development of Trade Finance & FX Transactional Business and monitoring it in conjunction with related branches/Business units of Yoma Bank.

Responsibility
The role holder is responsible for consulting & facilitating the requirement, documentation, regulation & compliance, and procedure of international trade payment methods/ transaction for the bank’s customers and branches.
Facilitating foreign exchange services process for the bank’s customers & branches.
Consulting & facilitating customers/branches for trade Incoming and Outgoing payment/remittance operational process.
Consulting & general reviewing required supporting documentation for Incoming and Outgoing trade transactions & FX for processing.
Handling query email and phone from branches & customers and providing customer support for Trade & FX products.
Qualifications
Bachelor of Business, Management, Commerce, Finance,Accounting, Economics and/or industry specific qualifications.
Desirable - MBA/MBF, CDCS/CITF/CTFP/CSDG
At least Three (3) years’ experience and knowledge of international trade payment/settlement operation and foreign exchange services.
Understanding the requirement, documentation, and procedure of international trade payment/ remittance (Inward & Outward) transactions
Understanding of compliance and regulatory requirements by Central Bank of Myanmar and other instructions from regulators regarding trade payment & settlement.
Open to
Male/Female
