MARGA GROUP

Assistant Property Officer

Yankin | Yangon
450,000 MMK
Job description

Conduct the first inspection of the unit with the Project Development Team (for unsold unit only) and Engineering Maintenance Team, Housekeeping team and Security Team and list out any defects found.
Receive instructions directly from the Customer Service Manager. Ensure to check and overall, the property of residents; acts as a liaison between the residents and the various departments.Coordinate with Customer Service Manager for necessary tasks.

Responsibility
Supervise and oversee the daily activities of the customer services executives.
Provide guidance, support, and training to subordinate staff on property management processes and procedures.
Develop and implement standard operating procedures for property management activities and processes.
Prepare the monthly duty roster and arrange duty relievers as needed.
Collect leave forms from subordinates and submit them for approval.
Ensure that any issues or concerns raised during the round checks are documented and addressed in a timely manner.
Respond to any emergencies or incidents that may occur during the round checks and coordinate with relevant departments to resolve the issues.
Monitor the performance of housekeeping, security, and engineering staff and provide feedback to the management team.
Check for any water leaks, damages, or defects in the building structure, electrical systems, plumbing, and other facilities in coordination with Engineering Team.
Conduct round checks at least once a week in all common areas, including lobbies, corridors, elevators, staircases, parking lots, and other facilities.
